FAQs

Everything You Need to Know

Find answers to common questions about the hackathon, application process, and participation details

What is the team size requirement?

Each team must consist of exactly 3 members. This is a mandatory requirement for participation. All team members must be registered and approved before the event begins.

What are the important dates?

Key dates for the hackathon:

  • Application Deadline: December 2, 2025
  • Selected Teams Notification: December 3-4, 2025
  • Hackathon Dates: December 5-7, 2025 (3 days)

What expenses are covered for selected teams?

All selected teams receive the following benefits completely free:

  • Full accommodation for all 3 days
  • Transportation to and from the venue
  • All meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks)
  • Expert mentorship sessions
  • Networking opportunities
  • Participation certificate

How is the prize pool distributed?

The total prize pool of ₺150,000 will be distributed among the top-performing teams. The exact distribution will be announced during the event. Winners will be selected based on innovation, technical implementation, real-world applicability, and presentation quality.

What kind of problems will we work on?

Teams will work on real-world challenges from the construction industry. These problems are provided by construction companies and focus on practical applications of AI technology. Examples include project management optimization, safety monitoring, resource allocation, quality control, and predictive maintenance using AI.

Do I need prior experience in AI or construction?

While prior experience in AI or construction is beneficial, it is not mandatory. We welcome participants from diverse backgrounds. Expert mentors will be available throughout the event to guide teams. The focus is on innovation, creativity, and problem-solving skills. Relevant experience can be mentioned in your application but is optional.

Where will the hackathon take place?

The hackathon will be held at Cyprus Construction facilities in Kıbrıs, Cyprus. The exact address and venue details will be provided to selected teams along with their acceptance notification. Transportation will be arranged for all participants.

How can I contact the organizers?

For any questions or inquiries, please contact us at emusoft.ai@emu.edu.tr. We typically respond within 24-48 hours. You can also reach out through our social media channels for updates and announcements.
